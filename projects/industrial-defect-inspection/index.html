Manufacturing challenge制造场景挑战

Smartphone component inspection combines diverse defect morphology, stringent miss-rate requirements, and very limited examples of rare defects. A single monolithic model was not appropriate for every station and defect type.

手机组件质检同时面临缺陷形态多样、漏检要求严格以及稀缺缺陷样本有限等问题，单一模型难以适配所有工位和缺陷类型。

Heterogeneous defects缺陷类型异构

Some tasks need image-level classification, while others require precise localization or pixel-level boundary measurement.

部分任务适合图像级分类，另一些则需要精确定位或像素级边界测量。

Rare positive samples缺陷样本稀缺

Production defects are intentionally rare, making it difficult to collect balanced training data for every failure mode.

真实产线缺陷本就低频，难以为每一种故障模式采集均衡、充分的训练数据。

Technical approach核心技术方案

01

Multi-task vision platform多任务视觉平台

Built reusable classification, detection, and segmentation capabilities rather than forcing all stations into one model pattern.

建设可复用的分类、检测与分割能力，根据工位问题选择合适范式，而不是强制使用单一模型。

02

Defect-specific modeling缺陷专项建模

Designed algorithms for audio-module solder joints, magnetic circuits, exterior appearance, and product markings with task-appropriate labels and metrics.

针对音频模组焊点、磁路、外观和产品标识设计对应算法、标注方式与评估指标。

03

Pix2Pix defect synthesisPix2Pix 缺陷生成

Generated additional defect appearances with Pix2Pix to supplement scarce positive samples and improve model robustness.

使用 Pix2Pix 生成补充缺陷样本，缓解正样本稀缺问题并提高模型鲁棒性。

04

Threshold and error trade-offs阈值与错误权衡

Balanced miss rate and overkill rate using production-oriented evaluation rather than optimizing aggregate accuracy alone.

面向真实生产权衡漏检率与过杀率，而不是只优化单一总体准确率。

Production engineering生产工程化

  • Standardized reusable vision capabilities so new stations could start from an established training and evaluation workflow.
  • Used station-level error analysis to separate imaging issues, annotation issues, and model issues.
  • Evaluated misses and false rejects explicitly because their manufacturing costs are asymmetric.
  • Connected algorithm outputs to pass, reject, and manual-review actions on the line.
  • 沉淀可复用视觉能力，使新工位能够复用既有训练和评估流程。
  • 按工位开展错误分析，区分成像、标注和模型问题。
  • 显式评估漏检与误杀，因为两类错误在制造场景中的成本不对称。
  • 将算法结果连接到产线放行、拦截与人工复核动作。
