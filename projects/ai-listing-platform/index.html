A one-stop multimodal system for non-standard second-hand goods. From images or minimal text, it predicts category, structured attributes, title and description, market price, and parcel dimensions and weight.

对标闲鱼 AI 发布能力，面向二手电商非标商品构建多模态大模型一站式发布系统。用户仅需上传商品图片或输入少量文本，即可完成类目、结构化属性、标题与描述、价格及物流包裹尺寸/重量预测。

Problem and constraints问题与约束

Classified marketplaces combine deep category trees, long-tail data, visually similar categories, unstructured user input, and strict cost and latency requirements.

二手分类信息平台同时面临深层类目树、长尾样本、视觉相似类目、非结构化用户输入，以及大模型成本与延迟约束。

Classification complexity类目识别复杂度

A flat LLM classification request across 1200+ hierarchical categories is expensive and difficult to control, especially for fine-grained and long-tail classes.

对 1200+ 层级类目直接进行大模型全量分类，成本高且难以控制，细粒度与长尾类目尤为困难。

Structured platform rules平台结构化约束

Generated categories and attributes must map to legal platform IDs and values without hallucination or cross-category contamination.

生成结果必须映射到平台合法类目 ID、属性名和属性值，并避免幻觉及跨类目属性污染。

Core technical work核心工作与技术创新

01

Cascaded category recognition级联类目识别

Combined SigLIP Top-K high-recall retrieval with Gemini multimodal reranking to balance recall, Top-1 accuracy, latency, and inference cost.

设计“SigLIP 小模型 Top-K 高召回 + Gemini 多模态大模型精排”架构，平衡召回率、Top-1 准确率、延迟与成本。

02

Multi-image representation多图商品表征

Encoded variable-length images independently and applied mean pooling to avoid collage resolution loss. Rich-text supervision and category prototypes reduced train-serving semantic drift.

对变长图片序列独立编码并 Mean Pooling，避免拼图分辨率损失；通过富文本监督与类目原型向量缓解训练和推理语义偏移。

03

Schema-constrained generationSchema 约束生成

Used the category knowledge graph and dynamic attribute schemas to map model output to legal IDs and values, with confidence controls, validation, hallucination filters, and fallbacks.

利用类目知识图谱与动态属性 Schema 映射合法 ID 和属性值，并加入置信度、结果校验、幻觉过滤及降级策略。

04

Price and parcel intelligence价格与包裹智能

Used Qwen3-VL Embedding, Milvus similar-item retrieval, and robust aggregation for market price ranges, alongside parcel dimension and weight estimation.

结合 Qwen3-VL Embedding、Milvus 相似商品召回与鲁棒价格聚合预测市场价格区间，并估算包裹尺寸和重量。

Production engineering生产工程化

  • Packaged classification, attribute extraction, copy generation, price, and parcel prediction behind a unified gRPC service.
  • Added cache, retry, timeout fallback, model routing, and observable logs for stable one-stop business integration.
  • Used BigQuery analysis and online A/B testing to connect model changes to listing and revenue outcomes.
  • 将分类、属性抽取、文案生成、价格与包裹预测封装为统一 gRPC 服务。
  • 配套缓存、重试、超时降级、模型路由与可观测日志，支持业务端一站式接入。
  • 通过 BigQuery 分析与线上 A/B 实验，将模型改进连接到发布与营收指标。
