Gumtree recommendation rebuild & AI-assisted listingGumtree 推荐系统重构与 AI 发布

Rebuilt a strategy-driven marketplace recommender into a modular, model-driven system and introduced multimodal AI to reduce the friction of creating a listing.

将策略驱动的海外分类信息推荐系统升级为模块化、模型驱动架构，并以多模态 AI 降低用户发布成本、改善后续推荐转化。

Context and challenge项目背景与挑战

Gumtree is a UK classifieds marketplace where recommendation quality depends on both user intent and the quality of seller-provided listing content.

Gumtree 是英国二手分类信息平台，推荐效果同时依赖用户兴趣建模，以及卖家发布内容的完整度与质量。

Recommendation bottleneck推荐链路瓶颈

The original system relied heavily on business strategies. Personalization was limited, components were tightly coupled, and algorithm experiments were slow to iterate.

原系统较依赖业务策略，个性化能力有限，链路耦合度高，算法实验和模型迭代效率不足。

Listing friction发布流程摩擦

Users had to manually choose categories and attributes and write titles and descriptions, creating long completion times and avoidable drop-off.

用户需要手动选择类目与属性并撰写标题、描述，流程耗时长，容易在发布过程中流失。

Technical approach核心技术方案

01

Modular multi-stage recommendation模块化多阶段推荐

Separated retrieval, pre-ranking, and ranking responsibilities so candidate sources, models, and features could evolve independently.

拆分召回、粗排、精排职责，使候选源、模型和特征能够独立演进，提升系统扩展性。

02

DeepFM feature iterationDeepFM 特征与模型迭代

Used DeepFM to combine memorization and higher-order feature interactions, then shipped multiple model and feature versions through controlled experiments.

利用 DeepFM 兼顾记忆能力与高阶特征交互，并通过受控实验持续上线多个特征及模型版本。

03

Multimodal listing understanding多模态发布理解

Combined listing images and partial user input to predict categories and structured attributes, improving content completeness at creation time.

融合商品图片与用户输入，自动预测类目和结构化属性，在内容创建阶段提升信息完整度。

04

AI-generated title and description标题与描述生成

Generated editable title and description suggestions, shortening the workflow while keeping the user in control of the final listing.

生成可编辑的标题与描述建议，在保留用户最终控制权的同时缩短发布流程。

Experimentation and delivery实验与项目交付

  • Defined recommendation and listing metrics that connected model improvements to user and product outcomes.
  • Coordinated requirement breakdown, experiment design, launch readiness, and metric review with product, engineering, and data teams in the UK.
  • Applied the AI-assisted listing capability across both Gumtree.com and OK.com.
  • 建立推荐与发布指标体系，将模型效果与用户体验、产品收益相连接。
  • 协同英国产品、工程和数据团队完成需求拆解、实验设计、上线准备及指标复盘。
  • 推动 AI 发布能力在 Gumtree.com 与 OK.com 两个平台落地。
