Retinal ReID for identity authentication眼底 ReID 身份认证

Built an end-to-end retinal identity matching pipeline that combines anatomical segmentation with metric learning, turning medical images into stable embeddings for authentication.

Problem framing问题定义

Retinal images contain rich vascular and anatomical patterns that can serve as biometric signals, but acquisition variation and medical-image noise make direct pixel-level comparison unreliable.

眼底图像包含丰富、相对稳定的血管及解剖结构，可作为生物特征；但采集差异与医疗影像噪声使直接像素比较难以获得可靠结果。

Appearance variation外观变化

Illumination, field of view, device differences, and image quality can shift the visual distribution of the same identity.

光照、视野、设备和成像质量差异，会造成同一身份图像的视觉分布变化。

Identity discrimination身份区分

The representation must reduce intra-person variation while retaining subtle inter-person anatomical differences.

表征需要压缩同一人的成像差异，同时保留不同个体之间细微的解剖结构差异。

Technical approach核心技术方案

01

Anatomy-aware preprocessing解剖结构感知预处理

Used Res-UNet to segment the relevant fundus region before identity learning, reducing non-biometric visual variation.

在身份学习前使用 Res-UNet 分割有效眼底区域，降低非生物特征视觉变化带来的干扰。

02

Metric-learning representation度量学习表征

Adapted FaceNet-style embedding learning so retinal images could be compared through distances rather than closed-set class scores.

采用 FaceNet 风格的嵌入学习，通过表征距离完成开放身份匹配，而非依赖封闭类别分类得分。

03

Triplet-based supervisionTriplet Loss 监督

Optimized anchor-positive and anchor-negative relationships to tighten same-identity clusters and separate different identities.

优化锚点、正样本与负样本关系，使同身份表征更紧凑、不同身份表征更分离。

04

Threshold calibration匹配阈值校准

Evaluated similarity distributions and selected an operating threshold appropriate for high-accuracy authentication.

评估相似度分布并选择适合高准确率认证场景的工作阈值。

Engineering considerations工程化考虑

  • Structured the system as separate quality, segmentation, embedding, and decision stages for easier validation and fault isolation.
  • Evaluated the complete matching pipeline instead of reporting only the segmentation or embedding model in isolation.
  • Worked within a broader medical-imaging workflow spanning data construction, model training, and scenario validation.
  • 将系统拆分为质量校验、分割、表征与决策阶段，便于独立验证和问题定位。
  • 以完整身份匹配链路评估效果，而不是仅报告分割或表征模型的单点指标。
  • 在覆盖数据构建、模型训练和场景验证的医疗影像闭环中推进项目。
