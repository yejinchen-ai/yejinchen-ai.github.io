Recommendation iteration problem推荐迭代问题

Recommendation regressions and growth opportunities often require experts to manually join aggregate metrics, request traces, candidate sources, feature values, model scores, and experiment history. The process is expensive and difficult to reuse.

推荐回退和增长机会通常需要专家人工拼接大盘指标、请求 Trace、候选来源、特征、模型分数与实验历史，分析成本高且经验难以复用。

Deep causal chain因果链路深

A final exposure can be affected by candidate generation, feature freshness, multiple model stages, score fusion, and business reranking.

最终曝光可能受到候选生成、特征新鲜度、多级模型、分数融合和业务重排共同影响。

Multi-objective risk多目标风险

A local CTR gain may hurt diversity, freshness, marketplace supply, revenue, latency, or long-term user experience.

局部点击提升可能损害多样性、新鲜度、供给生态、营收、延迟或长期用户体验。

Technical design核心技术设计

01

Unified metrics and traces统一指标与 Trace

Joins impression, click, read, contact, revenue, and ecosystem metrics with request-level traces. request_id, user_id, and ad_id reconstruct candidate sources, features, scores, and reranking decisions.

打通 impression、click、read、contact、revenue 与生态指标，通过 request_id/user_id/ad_id 重建候选来源、特征、模型分数及重排决策。

02

Recommendation Agent roles推荐 Agent 角色

Metric & Trace, Insight & Hypothesis, Retrieval/Ranking Development, Guardrail, Experiment, and Evaluation & Learning Agents transform evidence into executable hypotheses.

Metric & Trace、Insight & Hypothesis、Retrieval/Ranking Development、Guardrail、Experiment、Evaluation & Learning 多 Agent 将证据转化为可执行假设。

03

Constrained change surface受约束变更面

Allows only versioned modifications to retrieval sources and weights, embeddings and model routing, feature switches, score fusion, and reranking rules—with snapshots and rollback.

仅允许对召回源与权重、Embedding/模型路由、特征开关、分数融合和重排规则进行版本化修改，并绑定快照与回滚。

04

Counterfactual replay and guardrails反事实回放与护栏

Replays historical requests and candidate sets to estimate multi-objective impact before shadow and canary rollout, with constraints on quality, diversity, freshness, latency, revenue, and marketplace health.

在 Shadow 和 Canary 前回放历史请求及候选集，评估质量、多样性、新鲜度、延迟、营收与平台生态等多目标影响。

Closed loop with AI Listing与 AI 发布形成闭环

  • Uses AI Listing categories, structured attributes, and multimodal representations to improve cold-start and long-tail item understanding.
  • Feeds recommendation exposure and engagement patterns back into listing-quality diagnosis and hypothesis generation.
  • Reuses category knowledge graphs, embeddings, schema versions, and model-routing infrastructure across listing and recommendation.
  • 利用 AI 发布产出的类目、结构化属性和多模态表征，改善冷启动与长尾商品理解。
  • 将推荐曝光与互动模式反哺发布质量诊断及假设生成。
  • 在发布与推荐之间复用类目知识图谱、Embedding、Schema 版本和模型路由基础设施。

Evaluation and learning实验评估与学习

  • Each hypothesis defines the target cohort, evidence, expected benefit, primary metrics, MDE, guardrails, and rollback conditions.
  • Validation follows offline replay → shadow → low-traffic canary → staged A/B rollout.
  • Successful and failed experiments, bad-case root causes, versions, and human review are stored as structured memory.
  • Historical similarity and prior negative outcomes influence future hypothesis ranking, reducing repeated mistakes.
  • 每个假设明确目标人群、问题证据、预期收益、核心指标、MDE、护栏和回滚条件。
  • 验证遵循“离线 Replay → Shadow → 小流量 Canary → 分阶段 A/B 放量”。
  • 成功与失败实验、Bad Case 根因、版本及人工审核结论沉淀为结构化知识记忆。
  • 历史实验相似度和负向结论参与后续假设排序，减少重复试错。

Project value项目价值

Moves recommendation iteration from expert-driven manual investigation toward a safe, evidence-based semi-automated workflow. The system shortens diagnosis and experiment design, improves cold-start and long-tail understanding, and makes both positive and negative experiments reusable.

推动推荐迭代从专家人工排查升级为安全、证据驱动的半自动工作流，缩短问题定位和实验设计周期，改善冷启动与长尾理解，并让正负实验经验都可复用。
