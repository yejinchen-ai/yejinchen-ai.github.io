About / 关于我

EN · Hi, I am Yejin Chen, currently an Algorithm Architect at 58.com / Wise Vision. With over 10 years of R&D experience in AI algorithms, my main research interests include Multimodality, Video Generation, Spatial Intelligence, and Recommendation Systems. I have led the research and optimization of AI algorithms across multiple platforms, including Douyin (ByteDance) and Gumtree. I received my Master's degree in Pattern Recognition from Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in 2014.

中文 · 我是 陈烨锦，目前担任 58同城 / Wise Vision 的 算法架构师。 我拥有 10 余年 AI 算法研发经验，专注于 多模态表征建模、视频生成、空间智能与推荐系统 的应用落地。 我曾主导过 Gumtree、抖音直播等多个亿级平台的 AI 算法落地与营收优化，并在 2017 年 ImageNet 竞赛中获得 VID 组全球第三。 我于 2014 年在 新加坡南洋理工大学 (NTU) 获得模式识别硕士学位。

Experience / 工作经历

  • [2024.4 - Present] Algorithm Architect @ 58.com (Beijing). Leading AI algorithm applications for overseas business (Gumtree.com), focusing on multimodal representation modeling and recommendation systems.
  • [2020.9 - 2023.11] Senior Algorithm Engineer @ ByteDance (Beijing). Optimized Douyin Live recommendation ecology using multimodal content understanding. Awarded "Beyond Expectation Contribution" by IES department in 2021.
  • [2018.11 - 2020.9] AI Algorithm Manager @ Airdoc (Beijing). Developed medical AI imaging frameworks and end/cloud inference.
  • [2016.10 - 2018.11] Deep Learning R&D Engineer @ Goertek (Beijing). Developed visual frameworks for industrial defect detection. Won 3rd place in ImageNet ILSVRC2017 VID track.
  • [2014.10 - 2016.10] Research Associate @ Rolls-Royce Corporate Lab at NTU (Singapore). Researched multi-sensor multimodal data for real-time engine fault prediction.
