Context and objectives项目背景与目标

Live recommendation is a high-frequency, rapidly changing environment. Ranking must respond to minute-level room dynamics while protecting user experience and the long-term health of the creator ecosystem.

直播推荐具有高频、强实时和多目标特征。排序既要响应分钟级直播间变化，也要兼顾用户体验、营收效率与主播生态的长期健康。

Fast-changing value价值快速变化

Static and delayed features could not fully describe the current engagement and monetization state of a live room.

静态及延迟特征难以及时刻画直播间当前的互动、付费与内容状态。

Competing objectives多目标约束

Revenue gains could not come at the cost of excessive negative watch time, low-quality exposure, or concentrated ecosystem damage.

营收增长不能以负向观看、低质曝光或生态损伤为代价，需要建立可衡量的平衡机制。

Technical workstreams核心技术方案

01

Minute-level real-time features分钟级实时特征

Built live-stream features and feature-importance analysis, then integrated the strongest signals into PK ranking to improve freshness and value estimation.

建设直播分钟级实时特征并进行重要性分析，将有效信号接入 PK 精排，提升时效性与价值预估能力。

02

Negative-watch-time modeling负向时长建模

Helped define negative watch time as an ecosystem metric and modeled it directly, optimizing user experience under explicit business-cost constraints.

参与建设“负向时长”生态指标并直接建模，在明确约束业务代价的前提下优化用户体验。

03

Content-quality signal fusion内容质量信号融合

Expanded a single feedback signal into an interpretable issue taxonomy and injected content labels into retrieval, pre-ranking, and ranking.

将单一负反馈扩展为可解释的问题画风体系，并把内容标签融合到召回、粗排与精排环节。

04

Risk and policy systems风险与策略体系

Used representation learning and clustering to identify coordinated abuse, while building reusable policy and analysis tools for faster governance experiments.

利用表征学习和聚类识别协同违规行为，并建设通用策略框架与分析工具，加速治理实验迭代。

Evaluation strategy评估策略

  • Used offline AUC and UAUC to evaluate ranking discrimination before online rollout.
  • Tracked negative watch time together with overall watch time and adjacent business metrics to expose trade-offs.
  • Used packaged reversal experiments to estimate the cumulative ecosystem impact of multiple governance strategies.
  • Segmented users and content categories to prevent aggregate improvements from hiding localized regressions.
  • 上线前以离线 AUC、UAUC 评估排序区分能力。
  • 同时监控负向看播时长、整体看播时长及相邻业务指标，显式呈现收益与代价。
  • 通过打包反转实验评估多项治理策略的累计生态效果。
  • 按用户人群和内容垂类拆分分析，避免大盘正向掩盖局部回退。
